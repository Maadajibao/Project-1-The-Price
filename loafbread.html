Pågenslimpa(loafbread)

Product information

Sweden's best-selling loaf. The classic Swedish loaf is baked on wheat and sifted rye flour,
which together with sourdough and syrup gives a lovely soft and juicy loaf.
Enjoy a Swedish classic for breakfast and snack!

Ingredients

WHEAT flour1, water, sifted RYE flour1, syrup1 (12%), sourdough on whole grain RYE1 and WHEAT, WHEAT gluten1,
OAT husks1 (2%), rapeseed oil1, GRAIN malt, starch, yeast, salt, dextrose, maltodextrin, vegetable protein (pea), flour treatment agent (ascorbic acid)
.1 Swedish-grown raw material. SWEDISH FLOUR 90% flour from Swedish-grown grain ground in our own mill.
