Background

What is today's Willys started in Gothenburg back in 1975. Here, Lars Lundin opened a small grocery store, LL's Livs,
where the goods were on pallets and the prices were almost ridiculously low.

The rumor about the different store spread like wildfire, the customers cheered and soon one store became two, three and more.

Not far from there was another West Swedish merchant with visions of selling good food cheaply.
His name was Willy Schlee and in 1986 he opened the discount store Willys Cash in Kungsbacka.
The store soon became a smash hit, too. The idea of ​​selling good food cheap was here to stay.
The two strong discount stores soon became standard-setting in Western Sweden and gave consumers the country's lowest food prices.

At the end of the 90s, Willys Cash and LL's Livs merged and became Willys. Soon after, Willys became part of D&D, which later became Axfood.
Today, Axfood is one of Sweden's largest grocery groups.

A lot has happened over the years, but we are still passionate about good food at low prices and have the same goal now as then: To be able to offer Sweden's cheapest food box.
This is probably why Willys has grown into Sweden's leading low-price chain with over 200 stores from Trelleborg in the south to Kalix in the north.

Background

ICA Gruppen's history

Already in 1917, Hakon Swenson started the Hakon company, which is the origin of today's ICA.
The idea was to let individual merchants with stores join together in purchasing centers in order to achieve the same economies of scale as
chain companies through joint purchases and other collaborations.

The ICA idea

ICA Gruppen's history is based on the ICA idea, which in turn is based on its own entrepreneurs in collaboration.
Each merchant owns and operates his own store and can meet customers through locally adapted concepts and offers.
At the same time, individual traders collaborate around, for example, purchasing and logistics to achieve the same
economies of scale as wholly owned chain companies. Basically, it's about using collaboration in combination with local adaptation.
As close to our customers as possible, and with a clear vision - to make every day a little easier.

From then to now

ICA dates back to 1917. ¨
Then Hakon Swenson founded the wholesale business AB Hakon Swenson (Hakonbolaget) in Västerås.
At the same time, he offered retailers to become partners in the company.

ICA as a company was founded in 1938 and was started to coordinate the operations of four Swedish purchasing centers,
including Hakonbolaget. The joint operations of four purchasing centers became an even stronger organization.

The modern ICA model was first created in 1972. Then ICA became a cohesive group and the ICA board had for the first time a majority of traders.
In the same year, the ICA agreement also became the basis of the modern ICA model.
The ICA agreement is a unique way of preserving the ICA idea by giving talented store employees the opportunity to establish themselves as their own entrepreneurs with little financial investment and risk.
